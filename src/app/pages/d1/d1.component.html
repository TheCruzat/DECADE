Next to the Fremont House was a bodega run by a great Korean family, they also did clothing repairs and alterations. We hit up that bodega for beer regular. The 501 jeans van lived in the parking lot of that bodega for more than 10 years, sadly by 2009 it had disappeared. The bodega closed in 2011, the same year the last generation of our colony left Fremont. Now, where we once played music, there are three condo buildings, and our beloved bodega is a Fire on the Mountain. To be fair, FotM has bomb ass wings.
